WHAT WE DO

Our Programs

Education

Naseem Academy

Comprehensive Islamic education including Quran memorization (Hifz), Tajweed, Fiqh, Hadith studies, and Arabic language. Our curriculum balances traditional Islamic sciences with modern educational methods.

  • Free tuition and study materials
  • Qualified male and female instructors
  • Classes for children and adults
  • Online and in-person options

Rizq Food Bank

Dignified food assistance serving hot meals daily and distributing monthly grocery packages to families in need. Special programs during Ramadan and Eid celebrations.

  • Daily community kitchen
  • Monthly family food packages
  • Ramadan Iftar programs
  • Emergency relief distribution
